Project StarLight

Project StarLight is my personal homelab! For those who don't know, a homelab could be defined as a group of computers that are used at home for server-like tasks (such as hosting services). I use my homelab primarily for hosting all sorts of random stuff I find useful, but I'm also actively developing infrastructure to support it, such as a documentation system. I'm also working to learn about a multitude of technologies from this, such as Linux system administration, automation, networking, and data storage.
